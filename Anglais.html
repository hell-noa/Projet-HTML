The Drum

Instrument grouping many percussions, the battery includes both symbals and barrels arranged so that only one person can play it. It is used in all styles of music and serves as a rhythmic basis. Only a few elements change depending on the style played, for example you can add barrels and double pedals. The instrument appeared in the 20th century in North America, at the same time as the birth of Jazz and all its derivatives such as the blues.
for more information about the history -> wikipédia

The main drum brands

Tama is a Japanese brand present since the 1960s in Asia which appeared in the 2000s in France, it produces all kinds of percussion but specializes in drums. It is used by many great drummers.-> wikipédia

Yamaha is a Japanese company that appeared in the 1980s, it is mainly known for these motorcycles and motorized devices, it was in 1987 that the brand bought the battery producer Premier then in financial difficulty-> wikipédia.

Pearl is a Japanese company created in the years 1949, it produces desks at the beginning then will specialize in percussion in 1950 and more particularly drums, one of his big hits is the "crystal beat", a transparent glass drum ->wikipédia

Mapex is a Taiwanese brand belonging to the KHS Musical Instrument group, the brand is founded in 1989. She is mainly famous for her snare drums as well as her skins. -> wikipédia.

Ludwig is an American brand created in 1909 by William Jr, a former professional drummer. The brand enjoyed great success in 1960 after Ringo Starr was endorsed by the brand. -> wikipédia

My favorite drummers

Yoshiki whose real name Yoshiki Hayashi was born in 1965 in Japan is a musician-composer. He plays three instruments, the guitar, the piano and of course the drums. He founded the group XJapan with Toshimutsu Deyama, a Heavy Metal group that would later evolve into speedmetal. The group enjoyed great success in the 80s, which earned it many tour of Japan and a world tour in the 2000s.
additional information -> wikipedia

John Bonham was born in 1948 in England, he masters only one instrument, the battery. In 1968 he became the drummer for the rock band Led Zeppelin. Sadly died in 1980 at the age of 32 and has since been considered one of the best drummers of all time due to his technique, his power and his influence, he will even be elected in 2011 best drummer of all time by Rolling magazine readers Stones.

phil collins was born in 1951 in England, he is a musician-composer best known as the drummer and vocalist for the rock band Genesis. He also plays several instruments such as piano, guitar, bass and lots of percussion. He is still today as one of the greatest drummers to have existed thanks to the large number of new technique brought to the instrument.
additionnal information ->wikipédia

Domnic Howard was born in 1977 in England, he is one of the rare drummers left-handed, he therefore plays on an inverted battery and has made it his trademark. He is also the co-founder of the English rock band Muse and since 2011 he is also the drummer of the group Vicky Cryer. Muse has now become a big name in world rock with more than 9 tours world
